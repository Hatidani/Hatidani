### 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a university student passionate about **bridging the gap between software and physical hardware**. By incorporating both of my majors into my projects, I focus on building smart, automated, and connected systems. 

- 🔭 **Currently Focusing On:** Programming microcontrollers (like Arduino) and developing custom ICT devices using Raspberry Pi.
- 🌱 **Currently Exploring:** Expanding into AI at the edge with **TensorFlow** & **PyTorch**, and building interfaces via **Android Development**.
- ⚡ **Goal:** To design seamless, efficient systems that react to their environment automatically.
